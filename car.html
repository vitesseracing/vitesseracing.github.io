Regional Finals Car

Our regional finals car, which we called 'The HammerHead' due to the unique shape of its nosecone which roughly resembled the head of a hammerhead shark, finished the track in a time of 1.5 seconds including the reaction time, and 1.3 excluding it. Front wing was made using Nylon, rear wing was made using Carbon Fibre, axles were made using Nylon, wheels are made using PETG
