Our Regional Finals Performance

Our regional finals were held at Suncity School 37D. This was our first F1 in Schools experience and we all frankly enjoyed it quite a lot. Our pit display looked great, fully branded with all of our sponsors' logos. Our total Race Time including the reaction time was around 1.5 seconds, while our race time excluding reaction time was 1.3 seconds. We ended up winning the Best Design & Engineering award, and we were also nominated for the Best Car Design award. Our Design & Engineering presentation went quite well, but our verbal presentation needed some work since the whole team fumbled during the presentation.
